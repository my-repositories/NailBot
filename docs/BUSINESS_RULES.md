# Business Rules for NailBot

## Working Hours
* Daily: 09:00 – 20:00
* Days off: Sunday


## Appointment Rules
* Duration: 60 minutes per appointment
* Buffer time between appointments: 15 minutes
* Booking interval: 30 minutes (available slots: 09:00, 09:30, 10:00 etc.)

## Cancellation Policy
* Free cancellation up to 24 hours before appointment
* Late cancellation fee: 50% of service cost


## Pricing
* Basic manicure: 1 500 RUB
* Gel polish: 2 000 RUB
* Full set: 3 500 RUB

## Other Rules
* Max appointments per day: 8
* Minimum age for service: 16 years
