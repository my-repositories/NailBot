# User Guide: Appointment Booking Bot


Welcome to the appointment booking bot! This guide explains how to book, view, and manage your appointments.

## Hybrid note (SaaS vs On‑Prem)

This bot can be operated either as a hosted SaaS service (many salons/tenants) or as an on‑prem installation (one salon). The booking experience is the same; your data is isolated within your salon/workspace.

## How to Book an Appointment

1. Start the bot by sending `/start`.
2. Tap the **«🗓️ Book Appointment»** button.
3. Select a **date** from the calendar.
4. Choose a **time slot** from the available options.
5. Enter your **full name**.
6. Provide your **phone number** (for reminders).
7. Review the details and tap **«✅ Confirm»**.
8. You’ll receive a confirmation message with the appointment details.


## Viewing Your Appointments
To see your scheduled appointments:
1. Send `/myappointments` or tap **«👤 My Appointments»** in the main menu.
2. The bot will show a list of your upcoming and recent appointments.
3. For each appointment, you can:
   * **«❌ Cancel»** — cancel the appointment.
   * **«🗓️ Reschedule»** — start a new booking flow.


## Cancelling an Appointment
1. Go to **«My Appointments»** (`/myappointments`).
2. Find the appointment you want to cancel.
3. Tap **«❌ Cancel»**.
4. Confirm the cancellation when prompted.
5. You’ll receive a confirmation: **«✅ Appointment on {date} at {time} has been cancelled»**.

## Rescheduling an Appointment
1. In **«My Appointments»**, tap **«🗓️ Reschedule»** next to the desired appointment.
2. Select a new **date** and **time**.
3. Confirm the new details.
4. You’ll receive an updated confirmation message.

## Appointment Reminders
You’ll receive two automatic reminders:
* **24 hours before** your appointment.
* **1 hour before** your appointment.
In the reminder message, you can tap **«❌ Cancel»** to cancel directly from the reminder.

## Main Menu Commands
* `/start` — show the main menu.
* `/myappointments` — view your appointments.
* `/help` — get help and contact support.

## FAQ
**Q: I don’t see any time slots for my chosen date.**  
A: All slots are booked. Please select another date or time.

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**  
A: Up to 3 months in advance.

**Q: Can I change my phone number after booking?**  
A: Yes, but you’ll need to cancel and rebook.

**Q: What if I miss the reminder?**  
A: You can still view and cancel via `/myappointments`.
